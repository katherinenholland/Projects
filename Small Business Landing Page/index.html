The Music Loft

Music. Life. Community.

Welcome to The Music Loft! Located in the heart of downtown, The Music Loft is a comfortable, modern space owned and operated by professional musicians. We offer music lessons in piano, strings, woodwinds, guitar, and bass, all taught by highly qualified music teachers in the greater Atlanta area. Join us in our cozy loft space to learn and be a part of the most innovative music school in the area.
